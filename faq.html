FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

📖 Where do the Bible verses come from?

The app uses a free public API from labs.bible.org to provide a random verse each time.

🌙 How do I change between light and dark mode?

Just open the menu (☰) and tap Toggle Dark/Light to switch themes instantly.

🎵 Can I play or stop the music?

Yes! You can turn on or off the background instrumental from the sidebar menu. (still in work in progress tho)

📅 How often do verses update?

You can tap the Refresh Verse button anytime to load a new random verse.
